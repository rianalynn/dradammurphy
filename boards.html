Dr. Murphy serves on the board of an organization focused on helping recently incarcerated men successfully reintegrate into society — reflecting his deep belief that health equity cannot be separated from criminal justice reform and social determinants of health.

A consistent volunteer at prostate cancer screening events and community health fairs across Chicago — bringing free screenings and physician expertise directly to Black men in under-resourced neighborhoods who may not otherwise access preventive care.
